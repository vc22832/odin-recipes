Boiled Fresh Octopus 白灼新鮮八爪魚

Description

Ingredients

  • Fresh Octopus
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp wasabi

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Put octopus in the pot
  3. Boil for 10 minutes
  4. Take out octopus and let it cool
  5. Slice octopus
  6. Mix soy sauce and wasabi
  7. Dip octopus in sauce and enjoy!
